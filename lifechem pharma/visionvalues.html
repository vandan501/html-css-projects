VISION & VALUES

Our continuous research and development stand us apart in the very own industry.

VISION

Lifechem Pharma is constantly moving towards the vision of having a credible position in the pharmaceutical industry and establishing its own strong footprint in pharmaceutical manufacturing and new drug research and development. Our goal is to provide a variety of pharmaceutical, intermediates and specialty chemicals that meet the highest regulatory standards. Our focus is on producing medium to large quantities of high-quality specialty substances. We intend to improve our domestic and international market expansion by providing a full range of well-built pharmaceuticals and satisfactory services.

VALUES

Lifechem Pharma specializes in producing high-quality Specialty chemicals, Intermediates. We use novel processes, mature functions and advanced technology to produce Specialty chemicals, Intermediates. In terms of cost, speed and quality, we have brought extraordinary value to Indian and corporate customers. We comply with the highest standards of ethics and integrity. We are aware that our customers value excellence and we want to reflect it in every aspect of our company. Our performance strategy, professional principles and business are based on the following values. Excellence and Ethics Commitment and transparency Customer-centered Quality and Innovation

OUR R&D CENTER

We have highly equipped the QC laboratory with the most advanced instruments, such as HPLC, GC-HS, FTIR, UV spectrophotometer and colorimeter . Our QC laboratory is supported by a team of highly skilled chemists. We follow strict and up-to-date guidelines and implement the most powerful testing methods to ensure that our products are of the highest quality when they leave our site and arrive at their destination.
